Forestation

Iceland has been almost entirely devoid of trees, the trees having been cut down since Viking times.

Grasslands

Iceland's vast land resources are well-suited for grass and grazing animals, most notably sheep.

Agriculture

The principal crops have been hay, potatoes and other root vegetables.
